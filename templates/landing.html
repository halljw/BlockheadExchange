The world of cryptocurrencies introduces new trading opportunities to both the casual investor and the active day trader. We believe in demystifying the technical nuances of trading these currencies, and with our tool we hope to show that machine learning algorithms can help guide your trading strategy.


To use our tool, select a currency and a machine learning algorithm. You will be able to try Linear Regression, Bayesian Ridge, and Lars. The features we use are closing price, the daily trading range as a percent of price, the high/low change, and volume.
